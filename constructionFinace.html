    Loans & Mortgages > Construction Finance

    This is a loan for estate developers and corporate entities.
    This helps the financial institution pay for the construction
    of their property and flexible payments with guided
    features are given..

    What you get?

  • Tenor up to 60 months
  • Competitive interest rate
  • Disbursement in tranches in accordance with agreed milestones
  • Strictly for estate developers and corporate entities
  • Equity contribution of 30%
  • Obligor’s limit is maximum of 10% of shareholders fund unimpaired by losses.
  • Additional collateral required
  • The developer to submit performance bond
