    Home Plan

    For rent repayment

    Savings & Investments > Home Plan

    This is a monthly savings plan with a concessionary
    interest rate. Withdrawal is not expected to be
    more than once in a quarter.

    What you get?

  • Interest payable on monthly deposit. Base rate plus 1.5% per annum payable quarterly
  • Withdrawal is not expected more than once in a quarter otherwise interest is forfeited.
  • Concessionary interest rate at a rate granted at the time of accessing mortgages
  • No prepayment penalties.

    • Various variants with their amount range:

    • Platinum (N10K -N49K)
    • Diamond (N50K -N99K)
    • Bronze (N100K – N199K)
    • Silver (N200K-N299K)
    • Gold (N300K-N499K)
    • Ruby (N500K and above)

    Monthly contribution, depending on the variant will be continued as loan repayment.
